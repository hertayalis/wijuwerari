Paolo Bernardini- Professional Career

Paolo was involved in a number of theater projects prior to landing his first on-screen role. The next year, he appeared in films and television shows such as Noi due and Ultimi della classe. In 2009, he appeared in the television series Distretto di Polizia. The next year, he worked in Non-C’e tempo per gli eroi. In 2011, he worked on the short film Lucrezia Borgia and the feature film Il delitto di via poma. From 2013 to 2016, he appeared in a number of films and television shows. Like the Wind, The Vatican, Francesco, Erminio Binto, Run from me, E-bola, Bubbly, Chernobyl, and Mannarino- Apriti Cielo are some of the projects.

The following year, he appeared in short films and television programs such as The Rope, In Search of Fellini, Rosy Abate: La Serie, and All the Money in the World. In 2018, he appeared in the short film, Megan. The following year, he appeared as Gianluca Vechiarelli in Very Valentine, Vincent Defois in Il paradiso delle signore, and Fidanzato in L’etoile. In 2020, he appeared in the TV series Luna Nera. In 2021, he played Alex Malvesi in the film Lost & Found, which was shot in Rome. The next year, he played Paolo in the film The Wedding Veil Unveiled. Some of his ideas were also pushed back due to the Covid-19 pandemic.
