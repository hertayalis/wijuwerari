Roxy Ahmad on Love Island USA: Who is the new cast member and where can you find her on Instagram?

May 17, 2024
blog

Love Island USA has controversy brewing. Cinco and Cash have hit it off, but Trina seems to be moving in. Now, a new girl named Roxy Ahmad might be adding another wrinkle to that relationship.

Three new girls are showing up this week.

One of the new girls is Roxy Ahmad and here is everything we know about her.

Who is Roxy Ahmad on Love Island?

The new girl, Roxy, had a short promo on Sunday night’s episode where she made her target known.

Roxy is a 24-year-old who works as a digital marketing account manager.

According to her LinkedIn profile, Roxy is from Scottsdale, Arizona, and works for Joybyte, a social media marketing company that helps companies grow their business. Before that, she worked for Blue Sky Media as a Content Marketing Specialist.

She graduated from Arizona State University in 2019 with a Bachelor’s degree in Public Relations.

When introducing herself to the viewers at home, Roxy said that she was “born an ugly duckling” and had to develop a personality “which I think a lot of the girls will be threatened by.”

How can you follow Roxy Ahmad on Instagram?

You can follow Roxy Ahmad on Instagram at @roxyahmad.

Her profile lists both London and Scottsdale as her homes.

Interestingly, she has some Instagram posts that make her arrival on Love Island USA a little confusing.

This included one of Roxy with another girl and the tag “she said yes.” And another comment that said “wedding season” and “we are already married.”

Roxy has just over 7,000 Instagram followers and that number will surely rise once she steps foot on Season 3 of Love Island USA.

Who will Roxy Ahmad find love with on Love Island USA?

Roxy made it clear who she is shooting for on Love Island USA.

Roxy commented right off the bat that she is interested in getting to know Cinco first.

This clearly won’t make Cash’s fans very happy. It is also something that might rub Trina the wrong way since she is looking at Cinco too.

Plus another new girl, Lei-Yen, is also considering going for Cinco.

It looks like the big man has a lot of girls gunning for him.

So, who will Roxy hook up with first? Tune in this week to see who ends up with who when everyone starts to couple up.

Love Island USA Season 3 airs on Tuesday, Wednesday, Thursday, Friday, and Sundays at 9/8c on CBS.
