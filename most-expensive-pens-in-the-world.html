You are probably wondering about the most expensive pens in the world. If that’s true, we have compiled a list of the top 10 most expensive pens in the world in this article.

Writing was the most priceless thing in our evolutionary past. Writing became the most significant method of preserving and disseminating knowledge over time as human memory became insufficient to keep track of everything. Initially, this meant painting shapes on the cave walls and roofs.

Man created the pen for that reason. You might imagine a quill while thinking of the historical writing instruments. The quill was just one of the pen kinds that existed before the fountain pen we use today.

We have used pens of different kinds for communication and artistic purposes for many ages. Due to the new technology era, we don’t use pens as frequently as we once did nowadays.

Making a note on a smartphone is considerably simpler than writing one with a pen and paper. However, not all pens are discarded in this regard. Imagine owning one of the most expensive pens in the world, you definitely will always want to write. Lol! Let’s look at the top 10 most expensive pens in the world.

1. Fulgor Nocturnus by Tibaldi

Fulgor Nocturnus is the most expensive pen in the world with a price tag of $8 million. The most significant Italian fountain pen manufacturer, Tibaldi, designed The Fulgor Nocturnus, a pen with a name that would amaze everyone.

When the pen is closed, the ratio of the cap to the visible portion of the barrel, which was designed in accordance with Phi’s divine proportions, is 1:1.618. It is even more sought-after because there is only one of it in existence and it is embellished with 123 rubies and 945 black diamonds. The Fulgor Nocturnus is first on our list of the top 10 most expensive pens in the world.

2. Montblanc Boehme Royal Pen

Another of the most expensive pens in the world is Montblanc Boehme Royal Pen. It is common knowledge that Mountblanc is one of the top businesses producing the most exquisite pens. Over 1,400 diamonds and 18-carat white gold make up this pen. Using additional 18 karats of white gold, diamonds, and the company’s emblem, the pen has been embellished.

A spectacular design featuring platinum, white gold, and a staggering 1,000-plus encrusted diamonds, the Boheme range continues to be a favorite among the most exacting pen buyers. Montblanc Boehme Royal Pen is second on our list of the top 10 most expensive pens in the world.

3. Diamante by Aurora

The list of the most expensive pens in the world continues with Diamante by Aurora in third place. The Diamante, a remarkable fusion of craft and art, was designed by the premium pen maker Aurora, which has its headquarters in Italy. This pen is a work of art made of solid platinum and has an 18-karat gold nib covered in more than 30 carats of DeBeers diamonds.

The 2,000 diamonds that are included in this pen were all set by the renowned DeBeers Jewellers. The fact that these pens are the only ones produced in this edition is one feature that sets them apart.

4. Heaven Gold Pen by Anita Tan

This pen, which is designed to resemble a planet in the solar system, was created by jewelry designer Anita Mai Tan. A pen’s design can make it appear far more spectacular than any other pen.

Its style is unique to any other pen on the market and is both imposing and attractive. It contains 43 carats of 2 billion-year-old jewels and 161 diamonds. Wow, gemstones that are really two billion years old! The Heaven Gold Pen by Anita Tan is fourth on our list of the top 10 most expensive pens in the world.

5. Mystery Masterpiece

The renowned Montblanc and Van Cleef & Arpels collaborated to create the Limited Edition Mystery Masterpiece, which gave rise to one of the most prestigious writing tools ever created.

The three options available to contrast with the 840 diamonds embedded in the white-gold body of the pen are sapphires, rubies, and emeralds. Only 9 of these were produced to commemorate both companies’ 100 years of producing high-end accessories. The Limited Edition Mystery Masterpiece is fifth on our list of the top 10 most expensive pens in the world.

6. Caran d’Ache Gothica Pen

The Gothica fountain pen, a limited edition writing tool designed to evoke recollections of Gothic art and architecture, earns Maison Caran d’Ache a spot on our list.

The body is made from rhodium-plated sterling silver, and the six sides are formed like a Gothic window. The design is adorned with a rosette and the Gothic symbols fleur de lis all over the piece. This pen is being sold for an astounding $406,453.

7. Caran D’Ache La Modernista Diamond Pen

The La Modernista Diamond fountain pen was created by the Swiss business Caran d’Ache as a stunning tribute to one of the greatest architects of the time, the Spanish Antonio Gaudi.

The body of the pen is designed to imitate a historic structure to honor the renowned architect. It is made with more than 5000 diamonds and 96 rubies, and it has an 18-karat gold nib that has been rhodium plated. The pen costs $275,000 to purchase. The La Modernista Diamond is seventh on our list of the top 10 most expensive pens in the world.

8. Montblanc Prince Rainier III Limited Edition 81 Pen

The Montblanc Prince Rainier III Limited Edition 81 is another superb design from the same craftsman. The fountain pen is named after Prince Rainier III of Monaco, who passed away at the age of 81, and is an homage provided by Montblanc.

The 18-carat white gold pen’s body is decorated with 996 diamonds and 92 rubies in a pattern reminiscent of Monaco’s coat of arms. The 18-karat gold nib is embellished with two rubies, and the pen cap’s clip ring has 19 more rubies. The Montblanc Prince Rainier III Limited Edition 81 is eighth on our list of the top 10 most expensive pens in the world.

9. Limited Edition Boehme Papillion Pen by Montblanc

This is a superb pen brand, as any true pen enthusiast will attest. It’s one of the most expensive pens in the world that is available right now, which can make it difficult to buy.

The Boheme Papillon Limited Edition Fountain Pen is another masterpiece from German luxury brand Montblanc. A total of 1400 diamonds and sapphires, expertly mixed to create the shapes of butterflies and plant tendrils on the body of the pens, are set in the collection’s solid white, yellow, or rose gold pens.

The pen’s top sparkles with a star-shaped Montblanc diamond that serves as its patented signature. But the cost also stands out. A stunning pen like this one costs a whopping $230,410. The Boheme Papillon Limited Edition Fountain Pen is ninth on our list of the top 10 most expensive pens in the world.

10. Perrier-Jouët Anniversary Edition pen by Omas

The Omas Perrier-Jout Anniversary Edition is the tenth most expensive premium pen. You can select the type of pen you want, and it is a limited edition.

The person who invented this pen first began bottling champagne. Collectors from all over the world are aware of how special this item is and how stunning the materials are. The Omas Perrier-Jout Anniversary Edition is last on our list of the top 10 most expensive pens in the world.
