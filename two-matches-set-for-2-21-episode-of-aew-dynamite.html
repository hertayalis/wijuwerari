Two Matches Set for 2/21 Episode of AEW Dynamite

July 28, 2024
blog

AEW Dynamite has been one of the most exciting shows of the company lately, and the upcoming episode of the weekly show promises to continue the same. With a couple of tag team matches already announced for the show, a new singles match has now been made official. 

Ahead of their AEW Women’s World Championship showdown at AEW Revolution, Toni Storm and Deonna Purrazzo will be in action on Dynamite. Though their opponents are still to be announced, both women being in action has lightened up the excitement among fans. 

Both stars were announced to be in action on Dynamite during the February 16 episode of AEW Rampage. Their opponents, seemingly, have been kept as a mystery which could be kept that way until the show kicked off.

AEW Rampage Lineup (2/21/2024)

  • Jon Moxley and Claudio Castagnoli vs FTR
  • Swerve Strickland, Samoa Joe, and Brian Cage vs Rob Van Dam, Hook, and ‘Hangman’ Adam Page
  • Toni Storm vs TBA
  • Deonna Purrazzo vs TBA

The current lineup for the upcoming episode of Dynamite is completely stacked. With some big names promised to appear on the show, it would be interesting to see how things play out during the show.
