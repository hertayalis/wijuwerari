Global We Got Married EP 11 Preview: Gui Gui Finally Meets 2PM Nichkhun; Taecyeon Jealous Of Emma

July 30, 2024
blog

Global We Got Married Episode 11 Preview: Gui Gui finally meets her favorite 2PM member, Nichkhun, as he and other JYP family members come to visit the TaecGui couple for their housewarming party!

Gui Gui felt so shy and embarrassed when she saw Nichkhun coming, that she started laughing while leaning on Taecyeon's shoulder!

During the housewarming party, Taecyeon also became jealous as he watched Gui Gui's kiss scene with Taiwanese Actor Aaron Yan in her past Taiwanese drama, "Mysterious Incredible Terminator" (also known as Pi Li MIT or The Clue Collector). Taecyeon exclaimed at Gui Gui, "What's this?! Who's Aaron Yan?!!"

Fellow 2PM members Wooyoung and Junho, as well as JYP's duo group, 15&, were also among the guests of the housewarming party. During the party, Park Jimin and Baek Yerin dedicated a song to Taecyeon and Gui Gui with their beautiful voices.

The preview also showed a mysterious male guest who came through the front door to attend the party. Who could it be?

On the other hand, FTIsland's Lee Hongki and Fujii Mina will also show off their developing romance as they do the famous "Jack and Rose" pose from "The Titanic" on a boat in the middle of the ocean.

The Episode 11 preview also shows Hongki finally using the "Mina Coupon" and the two spending a romantic date at the theater and holding hands on a Ferris wheel.

Are you looking forward to see the hilarious interaction between the TaecGui couple and other JYP family members and to watch Hongki and Mina get deeper with their relationship?

Make sure to stay tuned for Episode 11 of Global We Got Married this weekend!
