Who is Shawty Bae? Viral TikTok sensation headlines tour shows

August 01, 2024
blog

Viral sensation Shawty Bae has landed her tour shows this year after finding success with her popular videos – so who is she and how did she get famous on TikTok?

Social media stars who enjoy creating content and sharing bits of their everyday lives have found success on TikTok, Instagram, and Snapchat and turned their interests into modern-day careers. One hugely popular creator is called Shawty Bae and we find out more about her background.

Who is Shawty Bae on TikTok?

Shawty Bae, whose real name is Jasmine Orlando, is a 20-year-old TikTok content creator and viral internet star born in Spring Lake Park in Minnesota on November 8, 2002.

She is a popular online figure on social media with 1.6 million followers on TikTok and 290,000 followers on Instagram.

From her lip-syncing to her dancing videos and hilarious skits, the hugely popular influencer made a name for herself under the online pseudonyms “Shawty” and “Shorty Bae”. Shawty was one of the original stars of Musically before the platform merged with TikTok in 2018.

Once she moved to TikTok, she continued to create similar content and collaborated with several influencers like Charli D’Amelio and Troy Zarba. Shawty’s distinct personality stands out in many of her videos because she shares her candid reactions to different trends and topics.

TikToker landed tour shows

Shawty’s solo shows in the United States are clear proof of her huge fan base and success as a content creator and viral influencer. Her tour stops include clubs and music events in popular party locations.

She held a meet and greet event in a Los Angeles shopping center earlier this year and hosted a summer bash at Volcan Nightclub in Houston on June 9. Her other stops as part of her tour include the states of Texas, Indiana, and Illinois.

On Thursday, June 29, Shawty was a special guest presenter at the Traviesa Party, an event for fans of Latin music, reggaeton, throwback school classics, and Latin trap.

Meet Shawty on Instagram

While Shawty found prominence on TikTok, her Instagram page is the best place to find the latest updates and details about her upcoming appearances.

She shares more personal bits from her life with her family and friends and what she gets up to in her free time.

In one of her posts, she shared a string of pictures of spending quality time with her mother and other family members along with the caption: “Had a good time with my family.”
